Steam Sales Description

This website is designed so that you can see exclusively what is on sale and free that you or someone else you know already owns on steam. I want to be able to provide people with an easier, and more covienient way to see what is on sale than on their friends game list than steam or other third party websites that also show the rest of the steam database.

In order to use this service you need your or your friend's SteamID or custom URL. You can find this by going to their steam profile website and then copying the last field in the url. Most of the time this will be a long number but it could be a phrase if you or your friend changed it.
