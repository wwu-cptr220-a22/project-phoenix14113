About the Website

This website is designed to cater to the gamers out there who are also frugal. We all know the feeling of looking at a game and saying "Phew! That is way too expensive!". The patient gamer will just wait until the game is on sale before buying it. If you put the game on your steam wishlist this is an easy process, but what if you like the game but not enough to put it on your wishlist? Or what if you want to "window shop" the steam store but you only are wanting to look at games on discount so that you aren't tempted to buy something at full price?

Website Overview

The general idea for this website is displaying discounted games on Steam. Steam doesn't have an incredible way of showing you what is on sale at any given moment. You have to manually search for a game or just find it on the main page to the next to see if a game is on sale. This takes a lot of time because you have to search for them each individually, got to their page, and then back to the home page for another search. And sometimes your search isn't at the top of the list so you have to look through a list anyway.

There are currently very few or no websites that specifically collect sales data from steam and show it in an organized fassion. There are websites that show you discounts but make you buy them on their website, and there are websites that just sell game keys at lower rates, but none that I can see link you back to the original website that actually made the sale in the first place. Not having to go through a third party payment method would be extremely useful and probably save people money.

About the project

This project uses an API to collect steam sales and display them for your viewing pleasure. Unlike other websites I will not show you any other categories other than steam sales. I will show your the header picture for the game, its name, the current price, and how much it is on discount for. No full priced games and no searching. This is purely designed as a way to brows games currently on sale. The only order to the games is that of collecting all of the sales games from the API. I hope this feels more like a randomized viewing experience than one organized and designed to show you the most popular items. Go to the "Sales table" tab to view this table of sales games

Why This Site

You should use this site if you are just wanting to chill and browse games on sale. There are no extra parameters to this part of the website. There are no text fields or searches you need or filters. This site is for the people who want a simple website and nothing else. Enjoy the scrolling with out pop out windows, ads, auto play videos, or any other kind of distractions.
